About us

We are an institution dedicated to promoting the knowledge and appreciation of African culture and afro-Brazilian, strengthening the identity, memory and traditions of peoples historically marginalized. Our goal is to educate, raise awareness and inspire society to recognize the African cultural richness present in Brazil and the world.

Our mission is to celebrate and disseminate the richness of African and Afro-Brazilian culture, promoting education, reflection and social inclusion. Through content, books and educational projects, we seek to strengthen cultural identity and African traditions. We act as a bridge between the public and organizations committed to the promotion of African culture, contributing to broaden its impact and reach.
