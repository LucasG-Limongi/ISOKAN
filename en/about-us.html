Who are we?

ISOKAN, which means union in Yoruba, is an institution focused on disseminating content with ethnic and racial themes. We want to help society recognize the African cultural richness present in Brazil and the world.

Our mission

We believe that technology has the power to transform desires into realities. We, as Isokan, wish to encourage inclusion, respect, unity, and appreciation of artists and works dedicated to addressing and debating ethnic-racial relations in order to broaden the knowledge of our users and expand the reach of this theme through the digital world.

Our mission

Our goal is to teach, raise awareness, support and disseminate books, videos, personalities... that enhance the different ethnicities. For this we have created a gallery filled with this type of content.
